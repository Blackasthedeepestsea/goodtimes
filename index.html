Be a better artist and make new friends now.

Art Classes taught by an award winning teacher.

Create and connect with other awesome artists of all skill levels. Welcome!

We have three unique art classes available.

Mandalas and Meditation

A short meditation is followed by working on color theory through mandalas, and working on drawing technique through creating your original mandala.

Every Sunday 5 - 7 PM at the National Art League in Queens.

Art Jam

Artists and musicians come together to create a good time for everyone involved.

Every Friday 7 - 10 PM at the National Art League in Queens.

Painting Portraits

Learn the system the fine artists of previous centuries used to create realistic portraits.

Every Sunday 1 - 4 PM at the National Art League in Queens.

How much?

We have two types of classes, community classes are donation based with a 30 dollar suggested donation and skills classes are 30 dollars for a drop in class.

Awesome!

Community Classes

Our classes are held at the National Art League, a non-profit dedicated to insuring access to art for everyone. This allows us to keep the costs of the class affordable.

$30 suggested donation/ class

  • No annoying subscriptions
  • No stress
  • No worry

Skills Classes

To get better as an artist involves commitment. I want you to see results. My classes that are focused on improving technical skills financially reward commitment.

$30 /class or
$100 /4 class package

Packages expire 60 days from purchase

  • See improvement in your skills faster
  • Share your interest in art with likeminded people
  • Participate in the yearly student show
